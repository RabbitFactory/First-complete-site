My DressUp Darling World

Presenting Marin Kitagawa

My Dress-Up Darling is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Shinichi Fukuda. It began serialization in Square Enix's Young Gangan in January 2018, and has been compiled into ten volumes as of September 2022. An anime television series adaptation by CloverWorks aired from January to March 2022.

My Chainsaw Man World

Presenting Power

Chainsaw Man is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Tatsuki Fujimoto. Its first part was serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump from December 2018 to December 2020; its second part began serialization in Shueisha's Shōnen Jump+ online magazine in July 2022.
